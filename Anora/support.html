Getting started

  • Color a mandala — open the Coloring Studio, pick a page from the mandala catalog, and tap a region to fill it. Every fill can be undone.
  • Color by number — pick a numbered page, choose a paint from the numbered palette, and fill the matching regions until the picture is revealed.
  • Dream up a page — type any word (a unicorn, a fox, a rocket…) and Anora generates a brand-new detailed coloring page just for you.
  • Draw freely — open an easel and sketch with pencil, marker, ink, brush, pastel, or spray, with full color palettes and a reference-photo tracing layer.
  • Collect & share — finished artwork lives in your gallery; save it to Photos or share it with family.

Subscriptions & billing

  • Anora Pro unlocks the full mandala and color-by-number catalogs, all drawing tools and palettes, unlimited AI coloring pages, photo tracing, and watermark-free sharing.
  • Plans: Yearly (with a free trial where available) or Monthly. Pricing is always shown in your local currency before you subscribe.
  • Restore purchases: open the paywall and tap “Restore.” Your subscription follows your Apple Account across devices.
  • Cancel anytime: iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Anora → Cancel. A trial isn’t charged if you cancel at least 24 hours before it ends.
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period; renewal is charged to your Apple Account.

Troubleshooting

  • Can’t save to Photos? Allow photo access in Settings → Anora → Photos.
  • AI page won’t generate? Page generation needs an internet connection — check your connection and try the word again.
  • Subscription not active after paying? Tap “Restore” on the paywall, then relaunch the app.
  • Still stuck? Email us with your device model and iOS version and we’ll help.
