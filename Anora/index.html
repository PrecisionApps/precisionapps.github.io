Anora

Color your calm.

Anora is a coloring book and art studio for iPhone and iPad — a quiet place to unwind that always has the page you're in the mood for.

What's inside

  • Detailed mandalas — tap any region to fill it; the color never leaves the lines.
  • Color by number — numbered pages with a guided palette that always turn out beautiful.
  • AI coloring pages — type any word, or pick a photo, and Anora draws a brand-new page just for you.
  • A real drawing studio — pencil, marker, ink, brush, pastel and spray, with custom colors and photo tracing.
  • Your little museum — every finished page and drawing collected in your gallery.

No account. No ads. Your artwork stays on your device.
