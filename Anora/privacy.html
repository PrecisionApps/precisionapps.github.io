Anora — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

The short version: your art is yours. Anora requires no account, shows no ads, and does not sell or share your personal data. Everything you color and draw is stored on your device.

What stays on your device

  • Your artwork — every coloring page, drawing, and gallery item is stored in the app’s local storage.
  • Your name — if you choose to sign your canvas with a name, it is stored locally and used only to personalize the app.
  • App settings and progress — stored locally.

Deleting a page or drawing in the app permanently deletes it. Deleting the app deletes everything stored by the app.

AI coloring pages

When you generate a coloring page from a word or a photo, that word (or the photo you selected) is sent securely to our image-generation service, used only to create your page, and is not linked to your identity or used to build a profile of you. Nothing else on your device is transmitted.

Photos

Anora asks for permission to add images to your photo library only when you save artwork. If you import a photo to trace or to turn into a coloring page, you pick it yourself with the system photo picker — Anora never scans your library.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple through your Apple Account; we never see your payment details. We use industry-standard subscription infrastructure (RevenueCat) to validate purchases, which processes an anonymous app-user identifier and receipt data solely to manage your subscription.

What we don’t do

  • No advertising SDKs and no third-party trackers.
  • No selling of personal data — ever.
  • No account, email, or sign-up required to use the app.

Children

Anora is made for the whole family. We collect no personal information from any user, including children. A parent or guardian should manage subscriptions and, for young children, supervise photo import.
