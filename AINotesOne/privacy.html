AI Notes — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

The short version: your recordings never leave your iPhone. AI Notes is built entirely around on-device processing. We operate no servers, require no accounts, and cannot see, hear, or read anything you record.

What stays on your device

  • Audio recordings — stored only in the app's local storage on your device.
  • Transcripts — generated on-device using Apple's speech recognition.
  • AI summaries, decisions, questions, outlines — generated on-device using Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models). No transcript or audio is sent to any server.
  • Notes, bookmarks, and app settings — stored locally.

Deleting a recording in the app permanently deletes its audio file and all derived data. Deleting the app deletes everything.

What we collect

Nothing, directly. AI Notes has no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no tracking, and no account system.

AI model downloads

Some on-device AI features (speech transcription models, speaker identification models) may download model files over the internet the first time they are used. These are one-way downloads of software components — no audio, transcripts, or personal data is ever included in these requests.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to validate subscriptions; RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and your App Store purchase receipt — never your recordings, transcripts, name, or contact details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details on their processing.

Notifications

Local notifications (reminders and offers) are scheduled on your device. We do not operate a push server.

Permissions we request

  • Microphone — to record audio. Used only while you record.
  • Speech recognition — to transcribe on-device.
  • Notifications — optional, for reminders and offers.

Children

AI Notes is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
